What is a QR Code?

QR (Quick Response) codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can store various types of data. When scanned with a smartphone camera, they can instantly open URLs, display text, connect to WiFi networks, and more.

Common Uses:

  • Share website URLs and links
  • Connect to WiFi without typing passwords
  • Share contact information (vCard)
  • Send emails or SMS messages
  • Make payments and transactions
